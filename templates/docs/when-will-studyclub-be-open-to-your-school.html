When Will Studyclub be Open to Your School?


First of all, thank you for your interest in Studyclub!

Studyclub's initial launch is only open to students from Queen's University, Western University, the University of Waterloo, and the University of Ottawa. In the coming months, new schools will be added if there is a demonstrated interest. Feel free to reach out to Michael Zhang (michael.zhang@queensu.ca) for more information!
