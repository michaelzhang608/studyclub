About Us

Late June 2020, students from across Canada were in disbelief when news came out that their semesters would be mostly or completely online. Students soon realized that they would have a tough time making meaningful connections with their peers. Being a student myself, I wanted to improve the "University Experience" of students from across Canada.

We'll be studying in from of our computer all day anyways, why not make it a little more fun?

What's next for Studyclub?
